Reading becomes navigation

A precedent is a proof: it shows that reading doesn’t have to move forward like a line. It can move sideways, loop back, or jump to a different node.

In the Garden of Forking Paths, every decision produces more than one future. This site borrows that logic: links are not “next pages.” They are forks.

Design decision

The layout is calm and minimal so the structure becomes the main interaction. The header acts like a map legend — a reminder that you are navigating, not scrolling.

A quiet fork (open)

Returning is not neutral. It doesn’t erase the path you took — it changes how you read the hub. The “same” page becomes a different page because your attention is different.
