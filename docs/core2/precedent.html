Reading becomes navigation

Reading isn’t only scroll down. It’s closer to moving through space. When you click a link, you’re not just turning a page, you’re choosing a route.

My prediction: the user has a little patience and curiosity. The site should feel like a quiet map: simple choices, small shifts, and a sense of direction.

Each choice becomes a path and each path changes what you notice.
