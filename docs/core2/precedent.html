Why this matters for the “Garden of Forking Paths”

Borges describes time as branching: not one straight timeline, but many futures at once. Twine makes that idea practical. The reader is forced to choose, and each choice produces a different reading — a different “now.”

This project keeps that the map is the interface. You enter through a node, return through another, and the meaning shifts with your route.
