Hierarchy as interaction

Hierarchy isn’t just “big title, small body.” It can be a behavior. What appears first, what is hidden, and what needs effort to reach — that becomes the interaction.

On this site, the hierarchy is simple: the hub is a clear entry, and each page offers small choices to continue. The structure is minimal, but the order still shapes how the story feels.

What you see first becomes what you think matters.
