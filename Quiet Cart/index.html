03

Reflection

What ChatGPT made easy

It made ideation fast. It was useful for generating names, flows, and early structure.

What it made difficult

It often defaulted to familiar e-commerce patterns and broad “clean UI” solutions.

Limits of the chat interface

Linear conversation made it harder to compare directions side by side. As the thread grew, useful ideas could get buried, and the outputs sometimes sounded more resolved than they actually were.

What the human adds

My role was to decide what mattered, reject generic defaults, and shape the interface toward a clearer point of view.
