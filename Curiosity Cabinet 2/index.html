UNIT 2 PROJECT

curiosity cabinet

a cabinet of everyday curiosities, secretly arranged as an emotional bingo board. each row holds a different feeling to discover.

😌 calm
😌 Why does rain make us feel calm?

The steady sound and dim light of rain can soften attention and make the world feel quieter.

😌 Why do quiet mornings feel special?

Quiet mornings can feel rare and protected, giving us a sense of space before the day gets loud.

😌 Why do warm drinks feel comforting?

Warmth slows the body down and creates a small ritual of care, which can feel grounding.

😌 Why do we enjoy sitting by the window?

A window lets us observe the world without fully entering it, which can feel peaceful and safe.

😌 Why do slow days feel peaceful?

When time feels less crowded, our thoughts often settle and even ordinary moments feel softer.

😅 social awkwardness
😅 Why do we wave back by accident?

Our bodies often react before our brains fully check the situation, especially in social moments.

😅 Why do we laugh when nothing is funny?

Laughter can be a social release, especially when we feel nervous, confused, or embarrassed.

😅 Why do we replay conversations later?

The mind likes to revisit uncertain moments and imagine better ways things could have gone.

😅 Why do we forget names immediately?

Names often disappear when attention is split between impression, anxiety, and trying to appear normal.

😅 Why do awkward silences feel so long?

Silence becomes heavier when we feel responsible for filling it, even if it only lasts a few seconds.

😭 nostalgia
😭 Why do old songs make us emotional?

Music can hold memory tightly, so hearing an old song can suddenly bring back a whole feeling or time.

😭 Why do smells bring back memories?

Smell is closely linked to memory and emotion, so a scent can return us to a moment very quickly.

😭 Why do we reread old messages?

Old messages can preserve connection, comfort, or a version of yourself you still want to revisit.

😭 Why do childhood places feel smaller now?

Our bodies change, but so do our memories. Places often shrink when imagination no longer fills them the same way.

😭 Why do we miss people more at night?

At night, distractions fade and emotions have more room to surface.

🤔 curiosity
🤔 Why do we open the fridge when we are not hungry?

Sometimes we are looking for novelty, comfort, or a small interruption rather than food itself.

🤔 Why do we google random things at night?

Late at night, thoughts wander more freely and even tiny questions can suddenly feel urgent.

🤔 Why do we click things just to see what happens?

Curiosity is often about testing possibility. The unknown can be more interesting than the answer.

🤔 Why do we get distracted while working?

The brain often looks for easier or more rewarding stimulation when focus begins to feel effortful.

🤔 Why do random thoughts appear suddenly?

Thoughts can surface from loose memory connections, especially when the mind is relaxed or wandering.

😁 joy
😁 Why do we smile when someone smiles at us?

Smiles are contagious because people naturally mirror each other’s expressions and energy.

😁 Why does laughing with friends feel different?

Shared laughter creates connection, making joy feel bigger than it does alone.

😁 Why do small achievements feel satisfying?

Even tiny completions can create relief and momentum because they make effort feel visible.

😁 Why do pets improve our mood so fast?

Pets offer affection, softness, and presence without complicated language, which can feel instantly comforting.

😁 Why does sunlight feel so good?

Light can shift energy, mood, and attention, making the body feel more awake and open.
